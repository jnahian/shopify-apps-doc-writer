## Product Overview
**One-liner:** The AI SEO agent that gets your Shopify store cited by AI assistants while ranking you higher on Google.
**What it does:** StoreSEO automates SEO work across a Shopify store — meta titles and descriptions, image alt text and compression, schema markup, sitemaps, blog content, and URL redirects — and tracks the results through Google Search Console and Analytics. It optimizes for traditional search ranking and for AI answer engines (AEO/GEO) in the same pass.
**Product category:** Shopify SEO app. Merchants search this shelf as "Shopify SEO app", "SEO optimizer", "image optimizer".
**Product type:** SaaS, embedded Shopify app (Shopify billing).
**Business model:** Freemium subscription with usage limits on products and AI Credits, plus add-ons.
- Free — 25 products, 200 AI credits
- Lite — $14.99/mo, 100 products, 1,000 AI credits
- Essential — $39.99/mo, 250 products, 5,000 AI credits
- Advanced — $249.99/mo, 10,000 products, 50,000 AI credits
- **AI Content Optimizer** add-on gates AI generation features (Image Alt Text Generator, Image Optimizer, Keyword Rank Tracker). *Auto* optimization is a further premium tier.

## Target Audience
**Target companies:** Shopify merchants, from single-operator stores to catalogues in the thousands. The pricing spread (25 → 10,000 products) implies both ends are served.
**Decision-makers:** Usually the store owner. In larger stores, a marketing or ecommerce manager. Rarely a dedicated SEO specialist — that's the point.
**Primary use case:** Getting products found in search without hiring an SEO or hand-editing hundreds of listings.
**Jobs to be done:**
- Fix SEO gaps across a whole catalogue without doing it product by product
- Show up in AI assistant answers, not just Google results
- Prove SEO work is paying off, with rankings and traffic in one place
**Use cases:**
- Bulk-generating alt text for a catalogue of images that has none
- Writing meta titles and descriptions for products that shipped without them
- Adding schema markup without touching theme code
- Tracking keyword positions and organic traffic after changes

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** A Shopify catalogue accumulates hundreds of products and images with missing or weak SEO metadata, and fixing it by hand is impossibly slow.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- Doing it manually in the Shopify admin means editing one product at a time
- Hiring an SEO agency costs more than most small stores can justify
- Generic SEO tools aren't Shopify-native and don't understand products, collections, and variants
- Most tools optimize for Google only, and ignore AI answer engines entirely
**What it costs them:** Products that never surface in search, images invisible to image search, and no way to tell whether anything is improving.
**Emotional tension:** Suspecting you're losing traffic to competitors for reasons you can't see, and not knowing where to start.
